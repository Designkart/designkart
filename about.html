Welcome to Designkart, an India-based design studio specializing in a diverse range of digital design solutions, including brand identity, branding, illustration, digital art, graphics, UI/UX, and more. With a proven track record of transforming challenges into customer successes, our founders, NID graduates, have collaborated with some renowned global entities like Google, Honeywell, Trident, Roche and HCL to help them achieve their business objectives.

STUDIO ARCHITECTS

Nikhil Kr. Rai

Creative Director & Brand Lead

A strategic powerhouse for branding and visual design, Nikhil Kr. Rai is an award-winning Communication Designer whose world-class pedigree ensures design excellence for every client. An alumnus of the National Institute of Design (NID) and a Gold Medalist from Banaras Hindu University (BHU), Nikhil holds vast experience in running impactful campaigns and building compelling brand narratives. His commitment to quality is unparalleled, having earned 17+ national and international awards, and his trusted experience includes leading the international corporate branding for Trident Group across multiple continents. Most notably, Nikhil is the designer behind the Indian Navy’s New Flag/Ensign and the official logo for the 30th India-Israel diplomatic relations, demonstrating his ability to handle projects of national and international significance. Nikhil’s specialties in Brand Identity, Creative Direction, and Visual Storytelling are dedicated to transforming complex client goals into simple, memorable business assets.

Sushant Kr. Rai

Director of UX & Innovation

A strategic force driving innovation and human-centered technology, Sushant Kr. Rai is a world-class Senior UX Designer and Researcher who anchors our agency with a decade of high-level strategic expertise. An alumnus of the National Institute of Design (NID) and a Batch Topper in Architecture, Sushant's authority is profoundly validated by his experience at global giants like Google, where he pioneers strategic AI and compute innovations at scale, impacting the world's largest compute infrastructure. His extensive background is uniquely valuable, including delivering solutions for leading enterprises such as Honeywell (Oil & Gas), Roche (Medical), HCL, and numerous high-growth startups. Sushant excels at balancing desirability, feasibility, and viability to create maximum client value. His core expertise spans UX Strategy, AI/ML UX, and Design Systems, ensuring the transformation of your most challenging enterprise problems into frictionless, intuitive, and profitable end-to-end user experiences.
