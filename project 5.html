BHU Sports Board Logo


Logo Design • Branding

Banaras Hindu University's (BHU) University Sports Board recently conducted a logo design competition aimed at establishing a distinctive visual symbol for their organization. The outcome was exceptionally gratifying for us as our design emerged as the recipient of the first prize. What's even more noteworthy is that our creation has now been chosen as the official emblem of the BHU Sports Board. This emblem is set to become an integral part of the board's identity, serving as the face of the organization in all its official endeavors and activities.

We're immensely honored to have our design selected, and it's a testament to our creative efforts and dedication. The logo will not only represent the BHU Sports Board but also embody the spirit and values of the university, further solidifying our connection with this esteemed institution.
