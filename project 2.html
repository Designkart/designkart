Logo Design for India-Israel 30th Diplomatic Anniversary


Logo Design • Branding

30 Years of India - Israel Friendship: In 2022, the celebration marked 30 years of diplomatic relations between Israel and India. Since its formalization in 1992, the relationship between the two countries had developed into a close partnership across various domains, including agriculture, academia, research, medicine, sciences, culture, and more. As the Embassy of Israel in India prepared for this significant anniversary, they hosted a Logo Design Contest for citizens from India and Israel. The contest invited individuals to create a logo that best represented the rich historical ties and the contemporary, innovative, and forward-looking collaboration between the two nations.

" In the logo, I showed the symbol of both countries together, which represents that they have developed and grown into a close partnership in many different areas including agriculture, academia, research, medicine, sciences, culture and more. By using Star from Israel’s national flag and Ashoka Chakra from Indian national flag, the logo becomes memorable and easily recognisable "

- Nikhil Rai (Logo Designer)









































Both countries had launched a logo design competition in mid-2021, where entries were invited from students from NID-A from India and Holon Institute of Technology from Israel to participate in the logo competition. The winning commemorative logo was chosen through a joint decision of Embassies and Consulates of both India and Israel.
